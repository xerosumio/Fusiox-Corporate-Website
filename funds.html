Hong Kong Limited Partnership Fund (LPF)

Popular with Global Investors

The Limited Partnership Fund (LPF) is one of the most flexible and widely adopted fund structures for private equity and alternative investment strategies in Hong Kong. It is particularly favored by global investors looking for a tax-efficient and regulated fund regime.

Fusiox offers full-scope registration and setup services for LPFs, handling every step from legal structuring to operational support.

Key Services:

  • Fund Registration & Setup: End-to-end support for LPF establishment and registration with Hong Kong authorities.
  • Legal & Tax Advisory: Professional advice on LPF legal structures, tax policies, and compliance requirements.
  • Operational Support: Ongoing services for fund management, accounting, investor relations, and audit.
