How It Works

Simple, straightforward process to get your business incorporated quickly and efficiently in Hong Kong's dynamic corporate landscape.

Our Simple 4-Step Process

  • Data Collection
  • Auto-Fill & Preparation
  • Review & Approval
  • Submission & Completion
1

Data Collection

We gather all necessary information about your business through our simple online questionnaire.

What we collect:

  • • Business name preferences
  • • Business structure type
  • • Owner/member information
  • • Registered agent details
2

Auto-Fill & Preparation

Our system automatically prepares all required documents and forms based on your information.

We prepare:

  • • Articles of Incorporation/Organization
  • • State-specific forms
  • • Corporate resolutions
3

Review & Approval

Review all documents for accuracy and approve them before we proceed with filing.

You can:

  • • Review all documents online
  • • Request modifications
  • • Ask questions to our experts
  • • Approve final documents
  • • Track progress in real-time
4

Submission & Completion

We file all documents with the appropriate state agencies and deliver your completed package.

You will receive:

  • • Business Registration Certificate
  • • Certificate of Incorporation
  • • Articles of Association
  • • Company seals (if applicable)
  • • Digital copies of all documents
  • • Ongoing information support

Typical Timeline

Day 1

Day 2-3

Review, approve, and file documents with the state

Day 5-7

Receive approved documents and complete business package
